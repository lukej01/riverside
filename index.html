Always Fresh · Quality Ingredients

Riverside Pizza is dedicated to
our high quality standards.

We understand that our success depends on the quality of food we serve and we couldn't give outstanding quality without investing in quality ingredients!

01 — Cheese
100% Real Mozzarella & Provolone
Grated on site at each restaurant — never pre-shredded. What you taste was cut that morning.
02 — Sauce
Stanislaus Pizza Sauce
The quality leader in the industry. Packed from fresh tomatoes only — never concentrated. Our homemade spice mix is blended in daily.
03 — Dough
Handmade Every Morning
Made from scratch at each location daily using All Trumps flour, a General Mills Gold Medal product.
04 — Pepperoni
Hormel Pepperoni Only
The only pepperoni we use — one of the highest quality and most recognized name brands in the industry.
05 — Produce
Cut Fresh Daily
The produce is cut daily in each restaurant to ensure the absolute freshest product on every pizza.
