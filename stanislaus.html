Our Sauce Partner

Why We Use
Stanislaus

Since day one, we've used only Stanislaus premium tomatoes, the same brand trusted by the finest pizzerias in America. It costs more, but you can taste the difference.

The Gold Standard in Tomatoes

Stanislaus Food Products has been growing and canning premium tomatoes in California's San Joaquin Valley since 1942. Their tomatoes are vine-ripened under the California sun and packed within hours of harvest, locking in that sweet, naturally rich flavor.

Most pizza chains use cheap, pre-processed sauce from concentrate. We use 7/11 Ground Tomatoes and Full Red Tomato Paste, the same products used by award-winning pizzerias in New York and Chicago.

The difference is real. Our sauce is bright, sweet, and naturally balanced, never bitter, never over-salted. Customers tell us it's the first thing they notice about a Riverside pizza.
