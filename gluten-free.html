Gluten Free Options

We offer a gluten-free crust option so everyone at the table can enjoy Riverside Pizza together.

Our Gluten Free Crust

Our gluten-free pizza crust is available in a 10-inch personal size. It's made with a rice flour blend and is certified gluten-free before preparation.

  • Available at all 8 locations
  • 10-inch personal size
  • Rice flour blend crust
  • Any toppings available
  • Same great Stanislaus sauce

Other GF-Friendly Items

Beyond our gluten-free crust, several items on our menu are naturally gluten-free or can be modified.

  • Wings (no breading)
  • Garden Salad
  • Greek Salad
  • Chef Salad
  • Most dipping sauces

Important Allergen Notice

While we offer gluten-free crust, our kitchens are not gluten-free environments. Pizzas are prepared in the same area as our regular dough. We cannot guarantee zero cross-contamination. Please inform your server of any severe allergies.
