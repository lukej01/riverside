Our Story

Family Owned &
Proudly Local

Since 1999, the Thompson family has been serving Metro Atlanta the freshest, most delicious pizza made with love, real ingredients, and recipes perfected over 25 years.

How It Started

A Pizza Dream Born in Georgia

In 1999, Al and Sandy Thompson opened the very first Riverside Pizza in Lawrenceville, Georgia. Their vision was simple: serve the freshest, highest-quality pizza with genuine hospitality.

What began as a single storefront has grown into eight thriving locations across Metro Atlanta. But one thing has never changed: every pizza is still made with the same care, the same fresh ingredients, and the same family recipes.

Al and Sandy remain deeply involved in every location, ensuring that the quality and warmth that built Riverside Pizza continues in every single pie.

What We Stand For

Three principles. 25 years.

Every decision we make comes back to the same values that opened our first store in 1999.

01

Community First

We sponsor local teams, hire local staff, and source local produce. Eight locations, eight neighborhoods we call home.

02

Real Ingredients Only

100% real mozzarella, hand-grated on site. Premium Stanislaus tomatoes. Dough made from scratch every morning. We never cut corners.

03

Made with Love

Every pizza is hand-tossed, every sub is oven-baked, and every order is prepared with the same care you'd give your own family. Al and Sandy Thompson remain personally involved in every location to make sure of it.

Our Secret Ingredient

Stanislaus Premium Tomatoes

While most pizza shops use cheap canned sauce, we insist on Stanislaus, the gold standard. Grown in California's San Joaquin Valley, vine-ripened and packed within hours of harvest.

The result? A rich, sweet, naturally balanced sauce you can taste in every bite. It's more expensive, but our customers are worth it.
